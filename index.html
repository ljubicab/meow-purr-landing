How to feed a cat?

Choosing a cat food

An average sized adult cat needs around 250 calories a day. Your cat’s particular calorie needs will depend on its size, weight, and activity level.

Feeding a combination of canned and dry food

Using a combination of wet and dry food can help your cat stay hydrated better than dry food alone.

Purchasing high quality food

Just like human foods, quality cat food will have a healthy balance of protein, fat, carbohydrates, vitamins, and minerals.

Avoiding giving your cat too many treats

It’s fine to feed your cat commercial treats or canned salmon or tuna, but only in moderation.

Considering feeding your cat homemade food

Homemade food can offer cats fresher, wholesome ingredients that don’t include the additives and preservatives found in most commercial cat food.

Establishing a feeding routine

A regular feeding routine will help keep your cat happy and comfortable.
